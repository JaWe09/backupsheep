Frequently asked questions

Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our support team by sending us an email and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.

How do I get paid?
Commission fees pay out after 30 days, on the first business day of each month. We pay our affiliates via Paypal or Wise Money Transfer and have no minimums to hit before you receive your money.
When do I get paid?
Every single month you have at least one referral, we send you money. There are no minimum payouts. Referrals are paid out after 30 days on the following 1st of the month. So if you refer someone on May 25th, you’ll get paid on July 1st.
How do I track my referrals?
We use easy-to-use dashboard from our affiliate partner Rewardful.
What if I forgot to ask someone to use my URL?
Email us who you referred and when, and we’ll manually attribute the affiliate conversion to you.
Do I need to be a BackupSheep customer?
Nope! While it’s a great advantage to use BackupSheep yourself so you can talk about how it works and how it personally benefits your website and business, it’s not a requirement.
How do I promote BackupSheep?
Any way you’d like! You’re free to talk about BackupSheep on your website, newsletter, video channel, social media, or anywhere else.
